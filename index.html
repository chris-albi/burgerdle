HOW TO PLAY

The game

Guess the burgerdle in 6 tries.
  • Each guess must be a valid fast food item.
  • Incorrect guesses will help guide you to the target item.

If you guess the target item, you win!

A new burgerdle is available every day!

Examples

If your guess has a shared attribute with the target item, the bubble will turn green.

If the two don't match, the bubble will turn red.

If you only match some of the ingredients of the item, the bubble will turn yellow and tell you which match.
